About Me

My name is Jyotiraditya Kumar Singh. I was born in Aurangabad, Bihar (my maternal home). Till 2016, I used to live in Udvada, Valsad, Gujarat. After that I shifted back to my native place, Supaul, Bihar. I stayed there till the completion of my class 10th examinations in 2018. I relocated to Patna, Bihar for my higher secondary studies. In 2021, I passed the IISER Aptitude test, which granted me admission to the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Mohali. I am currently in my final year of the 5-year BS-MS (Bachelor of Science and Master of Science) programme, majoring in Physics.
